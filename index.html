Brief Biography

Dr. Jing Zhang was born in a beautiful warm small city Wuhu located in the middle east of China. He received the B.S. degree in Engineering from Anhui University, Hefei, China, in 2003, the M.S. degree in Computer Science from the Graduate University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China, in 2006, and the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Hefei University of Technology, Hefei, China, in 2015. Before he studied for his Ph.D. degree, he was a senior software engineer in the R&D department in the Anhui USTC iFLYTEK company.
